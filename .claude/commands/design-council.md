# /design-council — convene the Design Council

**What it does:** Convenes the Design Council on a feature spec — Stage 6 of the lifecycle, **mandatory for every Sensitive change**. Applies the full lens panel (CONVENTIONS §4) to the whole feature, not one dimension.

**Inputs:** a feature spec that has already passed Ethical review (Stage 4) and Architecture review (Stage 5).

**What it produces:** a Council record that, **for each engaged lens, states: agreement · conflicts · tradeoffs · open questions · recommendation** (CONVENTIONS §4). We apply the named principle as a lens — we do **not** roleplay the thinkers. A blocking conflict returns the change to the relevant earlier stage; it does not proceed on a tie.

**Triggers:** the `design-council` skill/workflow · Stage 6 of `.claude/workflows/feature-lifecycle.md`.
